== Description == This template renders a beautiful, interactive timeline using a Lua module. The timeline's appearance and functionality are a direct port of the Fandom Wiki Timeline Builder tool. You will be able to freely add events, alter the range of the timeline, change the name of events, or even embed links to other pages in the wiki. == Syntax == 
{{Timeline|data={...}}}
== Usage == To use this template, you will need to take 4 steps. # navigate to https://medeina-pysis.github.io/Fandom-Wiki-Timeline-Builder/ # Enter your all the information for your timeline # Scroll down and click on the Copy Wikitext button # Navigate back to your article and paste the Wikitext in the desired location If you want to modify a timeline, simply go back to the source editor, copy the wikitext, and paste it into the paste bin on the Fandom Wiki Timeline Builder. It will automatically re-populate all the fields and events so you can mess around with, add, or remove events. just remember to paste the new wikitext back into your article. '''NOTE:''' The builder tool is used only to generate the data.
